### Podcast Overview
In this episode: Join us as we sit down with Curtis Coughlin, Ph.D. , an Associate Professor here at CU AMC in the Department of Pediatrics and The Center for Bioethics and Humanities. Hear about the amazing work his lab does on rare metabolic diseases in children, his service as a Pediatric Healthcare Ethics Consultant, and his journey into a career dedicated to helping patients from bench to bedside.
