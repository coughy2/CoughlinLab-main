## Seminar Overview

Our monthly lab seminar series brings together the research community to share latest findings and foster collaboration. This month, Dr. Maria Rodriguez will present groundbreaking results from our quantum materials discovery project.

### About the Speaker

**Dr. Maria Rodriguez** is a leading researcher in computational materials science with expertise in high-throughput screening and machine learning applications to materials discovery.

### What You'll Learn

- Latest advances in quantum materials prediction
- Machine learning models for materials properties
- Experimental validation of computational predictions
- Opportunities for collaboration and future research

### Who Should Attend

- Graduate students and postdocs in materials science, physics, or related fields
- Faculty interested in computational materials research
- Industry researchers working on quantum technologies
- Anyone curious about the intersection of AI and materials science

### Format

**Duration**: 45 minutes presentation + 15 minutes Q&A  
**Attendance**: Hybrid (in-person + virtual)  
**Recording**: Available to registered participants  
**Refreshments**: Coffee and pastries provided for in-person attendees
